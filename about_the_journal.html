About the Journal

Get to know who we are, what we stand for, and why researchers from around the world trust IJVRAS.

Who We Are

IJVRAS — International Journal of Versatile Research & Analytic Studies — is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal that welcomes original research from a wide range of fields, including science, engineering, technology, and management.

We started this journal with a simple idea: good research should reach people, not sit behind paywalls. Whether you're a student submitting your first paper or an experienced researcher looking for a credible home for your work, IJVRAS is built for you. We keep the process straightforward, the costs low, and the quality high.

Every paper we receive goes through a genuine peer-review process. We don't believe in rubber-stamping submissions — if your work needs revision, our reviewers will tell you clearly and constructively. If it's ready, we'll get it published fast. That's the deal.

What We're Here For

Research only makes a difference when it gets shared. Our goal is to give researchers — whether they're from well-funded universities or small colleges — a real platform to publish their findings and have them read by people across the globe.

We especially welcome work that crosses disciplinary lines. Some of the most interesting problems today don't fit neatly into one field. A study combining data analytics with environmental science, or technology with social impact — that's exactly the kind of thinking we want to encourage.

We also make a point of supporting early-career researchers. Getting your first paper published can feel daunting. We try to make that experience as smooth and supportive as possible.

What Makes IJVRAS Different

Genuine Peer Review

Every submission is reviewed by subject-matter experts — not just for grammar and formatting, but for scientific validity, originality, and contribution to the field. We take this seriously.

Truly Open Access

Once your paper is published, anyone in the world can read it for free. No subscriptions, no logins, no fees for the reader. We believe knowledge should be free to access.

Fast Turnaround

Our review process typically takes 2–3 days. Once accepted, your paper can be online in as little as 4 hours. We know your time matters, and we don't make you wait months for an answer.

International Editorial Board

Our reviewers and editors come from different countries and academic backgrounds. This diversity ensures your work is evaluated with a broad, informed perspective.

DOI Assignment

Every accepted paper gets a Digital Object Identifier (DOI), making it permanently citable and easy to find through academic search engines and databases.

Wide Indexing & Visibility

We work to ensure your published research is indexed and discoverable, including through Google Scholar, so it gets the reach it deserves.

Our Commitment to Ethics

We hold ourselves and our authors to a clear standard: honesty, transparency, and originality. Submitted papers are checked for plagiarism, and we have a zero-tolerance policy for fabricated data or results. These aren't just rules we post on a page — they reflect how we actually operate.

Our review process is double-blind, which means reviewers don't know who wrote the paper, and authors don't know who reviewed it. This helps keep things fair and focused purely on the quality of the work.

If there's ever a concern about a published paper — whether raised by a reader, reviewer, or author — we take it seriously and investigate it properly. Corrections, retractions, and expressions of concern are handled openly and without delay.

IJVRAS follows internationally recognized publication ethics guidelines. Authors are expected to ensure their submissions are original, properly cited, and have not been published or submitted elsewhere simultaneously.
